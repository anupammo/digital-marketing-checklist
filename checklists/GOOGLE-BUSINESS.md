# Google Business Profile (GBP) checklist framework

## 🔹 Google Business Profile (GBP)

### 📂 Account Setup
- [ ] Business name matches official brand/business name  
- [ ] Profile photo/logo uploaded in high resolution  
- [ ] Cover photo and gallery images optimized for desktop & mobile  
- [ ] Business description includes keywords, services, and CTA  
- [ ] Website link added (with UTM tracking if possible)  
- [ ] Business category selected correctly (primary + secondary)  
- [ ] Location/address verified via Google postcard or phone  
- [ ] Business hours updated and accurate  
- [ ] Services/products listed with details and pricing (if applicable)  

### 🎯 Content & Engagement
- [ ] Regular Google Posts published (offers, updates, events)  
- [ ] Photos/videos uploaded consistently (team, products, location)  
- [ ] Q&A section monitored and answered promptly  
- [ ] Customer reviews actively requested and responded to  
- [ ] Messaging enabled for direct customer communication  

### 📊 Ads & Promotions
- [ ] Connected to Google Ads for local campaigns  
- [ ] Location extensions enabled in ads  
- [ ] Conversion tracking installed on website  
- [ ] A/B testing ad creatives and CTAs  
- [ ] ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) monitored  

### 🔐 Security & Authority
- [ ] Business verified (green checkmark in GBP)  
- [ ] Two-factor authentication enabled for account admins  
- [ ] Admin roles managed securely (no unnecessary access)  
- [ ] Duplicate or fake listings removed/merged  

### 📈 Analytics & Reporting
- [ ] Insights reviewed weekly (search views, map views, website clicks, calls)  
- [ ] Top-performing posts identified and replicated  
- [ ] Audience demographics analyzed (location, device, search queries)  
- [ ] Customer actions tracked (calls, directions, website visits)  
- [ ] Conversion tracking set up (leads, bookings, sales)
