# Instagram Business Account checklist

## 🔹 Instagram Business Account

### 📂 Account Setup
- [ ] Username is short, branded, and consistent with other platforms  
- [ ] Profile picture (logo) is clear and high-resolution  
- [ ] Bio includes brand tagline, keywords, and call-to-action (CTA)  
- [ ] Website link added (with UTM tracking if possible)  
- [ ] Contact options enabled (email, phone, directions)  
- [ ] Category selected (e.g., Business, Personal Blog, Retail)  
- [ ] Connected to Facebook Page for cross-posting  

### 🎯 Content & Engagement
- [ ] Regular posting schedule (minimum 3–4 posts/week)  
- [ ] Use of Instagram Stories daily for engagement  
- [ ] Reels posted consistently (short-form video reach)  
- [ ] Carousel posts for educational/product content  
- [ ] Captions optimized with hashtags and CTAs  
- [ ] Engagement rate tracked (likes, comments, shares, saves)  
- [ ] Replies to DMs and comments within 24 hours  

### 📊 Ads & Promotions
- [ ] Ads Manager connected via Facebook Business Suite  
- [ ] Pixel installed for conversion tracking  
- [ ] Custom audiences created (website visitors, followers)  
- [ ] Lookalike audiences tested for scaling campaigns  
- [ ] Story ads and Reel ads tested for performance  
- [ ] A/B testing creatives and formats  

### 🔐 Security & Authority
- [ ] Two-factor authentication enabled  
- [ ] Verified badge applied (if eligible)  
- [ ] Admin roles managed securely  
- [ ] Linked WhatsApp Business account for direct communication  

### 📈 Analytics & Reporting
- [ ] Insights reviewed weekly (reach, impressions, engagement)  
- [ ] Top-performing posts identified and replicated  
- [ ] Audience demographics analyzed (age, gender, location)  
- [ ] Conversion tracking set up (website clicks, leads, sales)  
- [ ] Hashtag performance monitored
