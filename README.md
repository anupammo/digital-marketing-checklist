# 📈 Digital Marketing Checklist

**Digital Marketing Checklist** is the ultimate tool to grow your **Brand / Product / Business** with effective strategies and real-time analysis for organic boost.  
This project helps you implement **best practices across SEO, social media, and business listings** to maximize visibility, authority, and growth.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Real-Time Analysis** – Evaluate your brand’s digital presence instantly.  
- **Social Media Coverage** – Checklist for essential accounts (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest, Twitter/X, Threads, WhatsApp Business).  
- **Business Listings** – Ensure presence on Google Business Profile, Bing Places, Apple Maps, Yelp, Trustpilot, and niche directories.  
- **SEO Essentials** – On-page optimization, schema markup, meta tags, internal/external links, and content length validation.  
- **Authority Signals** – Reviews, ratings, backlinks, and social proof tracking.  
- **Exportable Reports** – Generate professional PDF/Markdown reports for audits and client presentations.  
- **Privacy-Friendly** – All analysis is performed locally, with no tracking or external data collection.

- Image alt text & optimization  
- Internal/external link audits  
- Social preview metadata (Open Graph, Twitter Cards)  
- Content length & keyword density checks
